Chapter 1: The Dark Woods

The woods are dark and a little scary. You hear all sorts of animal noises around you. What could they be? As you enter a clearing, you see a small black bat hanging from a tree branch. He looks tired.

Chapter 2: The Rushing River

You walk for what seems like hours. Slowly, the sound of a river begins to drown out the animal noises of the forest. The path opens up and you see the biggest river you’ve ever seen. It rushes by with whole trees zooming past you in its current. How will you ever cross this? After a moment, a hippopotamus floats to the surface, lazily chewing on some river grass. She eyes you and slowly floats over. “Hello, small hu-hu-huuuuuuuman,” She yawns, “I suppose you’d like to cross the river, hoommmmm?”

Chapter 3: The Cliffs of Climbing

As you walk along the path you start to hear a banging; soft at first but then getting louder. You turn a corner and see a large orangutan banging a stick against a rock until it breaks in two. He then picks up a large leaf and studies it for a bit before dropping it on top of the rock. Behind him is a huge rock wall, and the path seems to dead end into it. There is no way you could climb that yourself, but maybe he could help!

Finale

From high atop the cliff, the woods below don’t seem so scary. You turn around and see the path ahead of you. You walk for a bit and suddenly recognize a bridge ahead of you! That bridge isn’t far from home! Underneath it, you can just make out three shapes. You walk up and see your new friends. Though it is getting late and you need to get home, they invite you back anytime to the dark and now not-so-scary wood to play games with them.
